## Technical Details

### BMR Calculation Formula

**Mifflin-St Jeor Equation:**

For Males:
```
BMR = (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) + 5
```

For Females:
```
BMR = (10 × weight) + (6.25 × height) - (5 × age) - 161
```

### Nutrition Target Calculation

- **Calories**: BMR value (rounded)
- **Protein**: 1.0g per kg body weight
- **Carbohydrates**: 50% of BMR ÷ 4 calories/gram
- **Fat**: 27% of BMR ÷ 9 calories/gram

### Progress Status Ranges

- **Under**: < 80% of target
- **Optimal**: 80-120% of target (green)
- **Over**: > 120% of target (red)
